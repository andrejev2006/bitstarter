VICTORIA is free, personalized radio that plays music you'll love. The service available around the world with music you listen in any language.

VICTORIA can also be accessed through many media streaming devices.

VICTORIA had 5,800,000 tracks from 180,000 artists in its library.

The service plays musical selections of a certain genre based on the user's artist or song selection. The user then provides positive or negative feedback for songs chosen by the service, which are taken into account when VICTORIA selects future songs. While listening, users are offered the ability to buy the songs or albums at various online retailers.

Over 400 different musical attributes are considered when selecting the next song. These 400 attributes are combined into larger groups called focus traits. There are 2,000 focus traits. Examples of these are rhythm syncopation, key tonality, vocal harmonies, and displayed instrumental proficiency.
